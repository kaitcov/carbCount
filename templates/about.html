Why Carb Count?

Sometimes you need something simple to cater to your specific needs. As a type 2 diabetic, being able to count my carbs is an important part of managing my blood sugar levels. Equally important, is the accuracy of the information I log as well as logging that information in a way that is simple. That's why carb count only counts net carbs and calories, and all information inputted is verified by you.
